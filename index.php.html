FAQ

What is fix my cycle?

FIX MY CYCLE is a smart alternative to the traditional bicycle workshop, we provide bicycle service at your doorstep, helping you to save your time and money .

What type of bicycles do we service?

Fix My Cycle experts carry all the tools to service every kind of bicycle including: Road, MTB, Hybrid and Kids Bicycles etc .

How long does a service take?

Service time is based on the Package you choose, but most services will take approximately 60-90 minutes.

Do you do bicycle assembly?

Yes, we do bicycle assembly too, kindly have a look at it in special services.

Do you test ride my bicycle after the service?

Yes, ALL Bicycles will be tested by our certified expert mechanics,We also encourage customers to test ride theirown bicycles to make sure they’re 100% happy before we leave.

Do you offer pick and drop service?

Yes, we do have that service for few repairs which consumes more time.

Do you require a big space and what type of space for the service?

We don't require a big space for service, probably your car park, balcony or your lobby should be fine. We request the customers to provide a space where there no animal intervention and kids too, for avoiding any accidents.

How can i pay for the service ?

You can pay by cash or Paytm.

Can i change the service package after i book ? If yes, what is the procedure?

Sure. Write to us before 12 hrs of your appointment schedule to support@fixmycycle.in

Can i book a service on any day?

Yes. You can book service on any day. But, you cannot book the service for the same day. The service slots will be available only after 24hours from the time of the booking.

Can i change my address after booking the service?

Yes. You can update the details to support@fixmycycle.in, before 6 PM on theday prior to the date of the service.

How do i cancel the service appointment which i have booked ?

Kindly write to us at support@fixmycycle.in. You need to cancel the booking 12 hours before the time of your scheduled appointment.

How can place an order to service 2 or more cycles ?

We have an option on our booking page (additional bicycle) you can use that for the 2nd bicycle. If you have more than 2, kindly write to us in support@fixmycycle.in

Can i place a service request for additional cycles when the technician is at my place?

It won’t be possible because we work as per booking slots, where the technician will be having another appointment followed by yours which he has to adhere in time like yours.

How do i request for bulk institution service orders?

Kindly write to us in support@fixmycycle.in.

What is ADVANCE SERVICE ?

The ultimate annual service package. This package includes a complete disassembly, cleaning, and rebuild of all systems on the bicycle.

Is it possible to choose an advance service at door step?

Nope, this service can be done at our fully equipped service stations only.

How long does an advance service takes?

Depends on the packages you choose (24hrs to 48hrs), while you drop your bicycle at our service station, the delivery time will be informed .
